This mountain is 837 meters high and as a cable car. But it's not going directly to the top, you have to walk a little more. Visited on 2026-03-11. You can see it from the kingdom hall in Xuan Truong, its right in the backyard.

- Location: Link Google: [Xuân Lộc District, Dong Nai, Vietnam](https://maps.app.goo.gl/otgqf8vmnvjLHWtG9)
- Distance: 92 km, 2h03 [according to Google motorbike](https://maps.app.goo.gl/KNhJ6qBSW1bqy2yD9?g_st=av)  

## Hiking trail
I visited the place on 2026-03-11. On the starting point the woman that would park my bike for the day for 20 said there is a round course for 5 hours. 2 hours up, 2 down and 1 hour back. I checked on [alltrails.com](https://www.alltrails.com/explore/poi/vietnam/d-ng-nai/xuan-l-c/N%C3%BAi-Ch%E1%BB%A9a-Chan) and found 2 trails:
- [Out & back](https://www.alltrails.com/explore/trail/vietnam/southeast/chua-chan) 13.2 km, 1282m elevation gain.
- [Chứa Chan Mountain Loop](https://www.alltrails.com/explore/trail/vietnam/southeast/chua-chan-mountain-loop), 11.9 km, 796m elevation gain, 5 hours
