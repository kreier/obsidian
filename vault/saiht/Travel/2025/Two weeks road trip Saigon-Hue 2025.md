This was an idea since 2024 and in the planning since Tet 2025. Nga organized the stations and steps with the help of ChatGPT 2025-05-08 - LỊCH TRÌNH PHƯỢT TP.HCM – HUẾ – TÂY NGUYÊN – TP.HCM or LỊCH TRÌNH CHI TIẾT (12 ngày – 11 đêm). We started on Sunday, May 18th in Saigon, were in Hue after five days, May 22nd. Back in Saigon after 13 days on Friday, May 30th.
