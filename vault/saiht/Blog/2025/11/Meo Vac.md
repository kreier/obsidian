This part in the north north has great mountains and roads through it. Many tourists take the Ha Giang loop through this area. Done it twice.

## Getting to Meo Vac on November 2, 2025
Ha Giang itself is 6 hours from Hanoi, you might take a night bus. Or do a crazy drive through the rain as I did with Minh on November 2rd, 2025. 6 hours to Ha Giang, but another 6 hours through the dark, cold, fog, mountain to Long Cu, the northernmost point of Vietnam. No rest or food for us, only the bike! See [[Ha Giang loop a second time in 2025#11-02 The long journey to Lũng Cú 496.8 km]]

And if someone tells you about "professional" footwear, look at the woman actually climbing the steep mountainsides next to your easy road. They just have **flip-flops**!
