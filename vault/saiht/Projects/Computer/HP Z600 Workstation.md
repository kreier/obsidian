## History
I got this package with 24GB RAM and Nvidia Quadro FX580 graphics card on 2018/11/17 for 7.800.000 VND or about 300 Euro.
### 2026-03-02 Repair at RE COM
For two years I had an error in the PSU that shut down the whole computer after 3 minutes. The company RE COM repaired a few electronic parts on the PSU (rectifies, transistors, capacitors) and now the machine runs again!
